Mission

Cleaner streets. Brighter futures. Stylish spaces.

Collect

We map nearby garbage hotspots and organize safe, supervised cleanups with youth teams and community leaders.

Train

Volunteers learn sorting, cleaning, design, and product finishing skills that translate into real opportunities.

Create

We transform waste plastic into artistic interior products that are durable, affordable, and export-ready.

Process

From waste to wonder in three focused stages.

01

Collect & sort

Community cleanups, partner drives, and local drop points feed a safe, tracked collection flow.

02

Clean & craft

Materials are washed, shredded, and crafted into patterns, panels, and modular components.

03

Design & ship

Teams assemble products, quality-check each piece, and prepare for local sales and export showcases.
